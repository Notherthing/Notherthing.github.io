# Yuchen Huang

**Email:** [ychuang@stu.ecnu.edu.cn](mailto:ychuang@stu.ecnu.edu.cn)

## Biography

Yuchen Huang is a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University (ECNU), advised by [Prof. Chuliang Weng](https://chuliangweng.github.io/). His research focuses on high-performance, cost-effective, and practical systems spanning storage, computing, and communication, with recent emphasis on vector data systems and large-model infrastructure. He also has industrial experience in both engineering and research, including internships at RedNote, ByteDance, Ant Research, HFT, and Huawei.

## Research Interests

His research interests include hardware-software co-design for high-performance systems, and recently focus on vector retrieval systems.
